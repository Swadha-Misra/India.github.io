Serene Beaches

Serene Beaches are territories of dreams. They are our favorite refuges; they are a protectorate for the soul during spring, summer, autumn, and winter. They shed our tears, and they hear our pain. For surfers, it's the best place to meditate.A day at the beach is never lost time. When the warm grains of sand touch our hearts, we know we're in the right place. Close your eyes, take a deep breath, and let the beach invade your body.

  1. Radhanagar Beach, Andaman and Nicobar Islands

    Beaches in Andaman and Nicobar are indeed the most attractive and charming factor of your. The shimmering blue waters, smooth silky sands, lovely cottages, huts and shacks, and exciting water sports activities together make these beaches in Andaman and Nicobar an experience worth cherishing in itself.It has been ranked as the best beach of India and the 7th best beach in the world by the Time Magazine. Its white sparkling sands, turquoise waters, and plush green palm vegetation makes it a perfect place for a relaxed stroll.

    Best Time To Visit: October to May.

  2. Marari Beach,Kerala

    Marari Beach, a secluded little known beach destination in the Alappuzha, is located about 11 km from Alappuzha town. The fisherfolk of Mararikulam and their gentle ways of life are some of the attractions here. An evening stroll is one of the best hangouts here.

    Best Time To Visit: December to March.

  3. Varkala Beach,Kerela

    Varkala is a tiny hamlet on the outskirts of Thiruvananthapuram that has attracted pilgrims and tourists for a long time. Its beach is famous for its natural springs which are said to have medicinal qualities.

    Best Time To Visit: October to March.

  4. Cherai Beach, Kochi

    Famous for the rustic images of Chinese fishing nets from its sands, Cherai is one of the most visited beaches in Kochi. The seashells on Cherai Beach are unique and swimmers have been known to run into dolphins in the waters.

    Best Time to Visit:October to March.

  5. Golden Beach,Puri

    Also famous as the Puri Beach, the Golden Beach is a stretch of fine golden sand dotted with stalls selling sumptuous sea food and knick-knacks. It is popular with pilgrims who come to worship Lord Jagannath at the temple located nearby.

    Best Time to Visit: July-March.

  6. Bhogwe Beach, Kudal

    It is located in Kudal, Sindhudurg district of Maharashtra. Bhogwe beach is situated in Bhogwe village. This is an unexplored beach, hence, you can call it a hidden gem. One can’t stop admiring its beauty and the surroundings that it offers. There are various resorts and hotels that serve the best Konkani Food. There is a Dolphin Spot if you wish to see. You can spot white dolphins.

    Best Time to visit: October to February.

  7. Baga Beach, Goa

    It is named after Baga creek located in the Arabian Sea. Where Calangute beach ends, that’s where Baga Beach starts. This beach is famous for the nightlife as well as the amazing party life. There are various shops on the street side of the beach itself to shop from and pick up souvenirs. There are beach-facing restaurants that serve great food. This beach also offers a great variety of water sports.

    Best Time to Visit:October to March.
